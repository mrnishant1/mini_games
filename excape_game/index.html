Escape Maze

Reach the exit before the guards spot you.

Guards 10

How to play

  • Click the maze to pathfind, or use W A S D to move step by step.
  • Stay inside your light radius — walls fade in the dark.
  • Avoid red vision cones; they trigger game over.
  • Reach the green exit zone to win.
